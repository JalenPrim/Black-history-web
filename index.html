Who is George Washington Carver

George Wasington Carver was a agricultural scientist and an inventor. Craver was born into slavery. He had left home at such a young age to pursue education. Then later on he had gotten his master degree in agricultural science.

Facts About George Washington Carver

  1. He wrote the Carver bulletins
  2. He was the first black student to be accepted to Simpson College in Lowa
  3. He created the jesup wagon. It was a horse drawn mobile classroom and also a laboratory for farmers.
  4. He also helped Henry Ford make peanut rubber for world II cannons.
